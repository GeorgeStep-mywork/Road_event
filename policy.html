Policy

Shipping Policy:

We appreciate your interest in Offroads and want to ensure a smooth experience for all our attendees. Please note that Offroads is an automobile event, and as such, there are no physical products to be shipped. All tickets and workshop registrations are electronic and will be delivered to you via email. Upon successful payment, you will receive a confirmation email containing your digital tickets or workshop registration details. If you encounter any issues or do not receive your confirmation email, please contact our support team at team@offroadsmbcet.in for assistance or call +91 9544967011.

Terms and Conditions:

  1. Event Access:
    • Your ticket grants you access to the specified Offroads event and its associated activities as outlined in the event schedule.
    • Offroads reserves the right to refuse entry or remove any participant who violates the event's code of conduct.

  2. Workshop Registration:
    • Workshop registrations are limited and are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
    • Offroads reserves the right to cancel or reschedule workshops due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, participants will be notified, and alternative arrangements will be made.

  3. Ticket Transfers:
    • Tickets are non-transferable. If you are unable to attend, please contact our support team for assistance.

  4. Code of Conduct:
    • All attendees must adhere to Offroads' code of conduct, promoting a respectful and inclusive environment for all participants.

  5. Photography and Filming:
    • Offroads reserves the right to capture photographs and videos during the event for promotional purposes. By attending, you consent to being included in such media.

  6. Liability:
    • Offroads is not responsible for any loss, damage, or theft of personal belongings during the event.

  7. Changes to Terms:
    • Offroads reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Please check the website for updates.

Refund Policy:

  1. Ticket Refunds:
    • Tickets are non-refundable. Please ensure your availability before making a purchase.

  2. Event Cancellation:
    • In the rare event of Offroads being canceled, a full refund for tickets and workshops will be processed.

  3. Refund Process:
    • To request a refund, please contact our support team at team@offroadsmbcet.in for assistance or call +91 9544967011.. Include your order number and a brief explanation for the refund request.
By purchasing tickets or registering for workshops, you agree to abide by these policies and terms and conditions outlined by Offroads. We look forward to hosting you at our mega automobile event!
